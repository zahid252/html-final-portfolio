The Best Movies According to Zahid Khan

My top 3 movies of all time.

1. 3 Idiots

This movie is very inspiring this shows that how we can become sucess and happy in our life by following our passion.

2. Interstellar

Interstellar is a 2014 science fiction film that follows a group of astronauts on a quest to find a new home for humanity. The film is set in a dystopian future where Earth is suffering from famine and blight, and stars Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, and Jessica Chastain.

3. The Conjuring

The movie that started it all remains the tightest, scariest, and most creative of the bunch. In their cinematic debut, the Warrens investigate a disturbing possession at a remote family home in Rhode Island.
